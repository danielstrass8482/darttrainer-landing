Delete Account

You can delete your DARTTRAINER account directly within the app. Deletion through this page is not possible.

1
Open the DARTTRAINER App on your device and sign in.
2
Tap Profile in the bottom right corner.
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
4
Confirm the security prompt. Your account and all data will be deleted immediately and permanently.

Deleting your account will permanently remove the following data:

  • Your account and email address
  • All player profiles and game history
  • Statistics and coach analyses
  • Premium status and subscription information
Having trouble deleting your account? Contact us at
support@darttrainer.app
