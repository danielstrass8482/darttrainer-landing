2. Data We Collect

Account data: Email address and password (encrypted) upon registration via Firebase Authentication.

Health data (optional): Sleep, exertion and wellbeing — only if you activate this feature.

Technical data: No cookies, no tracking, no sharing with advertisers.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Manage your account
  • Store and display your personal dart statistics
  • Enable AI coach analyses (data is temporarily transmitted to the Anthropic Claude API, not permanently stored)

4. Storage

All data is stored in Firebase (Google LLC, USA). Transmission is encrypted. Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

5. AI Analysis

When you use the coach analysis or video analysis, your statistics data is temporarily transmitted to the Anthropic Claude API. This data is not used for training AI models and is not permanently stored.

6. Voice Output

The ElevenLabs API is used for voice announcements. The text to be announced is transmitted temporarily and not stored.
